About Kirikiriroa Loops

We are a Hamilton-based team who wants to share the beauty that is hidden in our little city. We started Kirikiriroa Loops at the end of 2022 with the goal in mind of sharing looping videos of local walks in and around Hamilton. We wanted to make sure that anyone can view these walks despite their location, age, and physical abilities. Our team is made up of Thomas, James, Susie, and Molly, who have collaborated from day one to make this vision come to life. Our goal is to branch out to other regions of New Zealand to showcase their own unique beauty.
